Privacy Policy

How Bristol Buzz handles personal information, account details, and website usage data.

Last updated: November 25, 2025

Introduction

At Bristol Buzz we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

Information we collect

  • Personal information you provide when signing up (name and email)
  • Usage information such as pages visited and interaction with features
  • Third-party data from analytics and ticketing partners

How we use information

We use data to provide and improve services, send promotional materials you consent to receive, and to comply with legal obligations.

Your choices

You can update your account preferences or email preferences in your account page. To remove your data or for specific requests contact support on the site.

Cookies & tracking

We use cookies and third-party tracking tools to understand traffic and provide features. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

Questions or requests about data can be sent via our contact form on the main site.
